FISH HOUSE

PROCESS OF CREATING A VISUAL IDENTITY

For this project, I developed the visual identity for the restaurant: 'Fish House', which serves sea food. The process began with creating two moodboards to capture the atmosphere of the brand. From there, I designed the logo, starting with sketches, and selected a suiting typeface. I also created a pattern and produced several mockups to visualize the brand. The goal was to create a restaurant with a cozy atmosphere and fresh sea food.

MOODBOARD
*restaurant

For this restaurant's brand identity I want to create a cozy atmosphere. I chose a combination of elements that give the feeling of both luxury and warmth. The color blue represents the ocean, giving a direct connection to the fresh seafood that will be served, while also creating a sense of calmness and elegance. Wooden furniture adds a natural, earthy feel, providing warmth and comfort to balance the atmosphere.

MOODBOARD
*visual identity

For the visual identity moodboard I focused on creating a balance between simplicity but elegance. The chosen images feature drawn fish and shells with a few textures, adding depth and character instead of just clean and straight lines. The color palette includes blue which symbolizes the ocean. I also incorporated some touches of orange to add warmth.

LOGO CREATION PROCESS

To develop the logo for Fish House, I experimented with many different versions and I tried out various details and levels of complexity. I tried different fish shapes, playing with both vertical and horizontal orientations and adjusted the amount of detail in each design. I also explored different placements for the text to see how it would look together with the fish.

This sign represents the visual identity I created for a fish house restaurant. It shows a beige background with a blue typeface and logo.
